## REDDIT POST (r/Truckers — educational)
Title: We built an AI that negotiates freight loads — here is how it works

I want to share exactly how it works because I think it is genuinely useful
even if you never use our product.

The AI does 4 things on every negotiation:

1. Calculates your REAL floor using live EIA diesel prices, exact truck
   miles, your actual driver pay, and overhead. Not a guess.

2. Anchors high and uses STATEMENT language not question language.
   "We need $1,950 on this lane" not "Can you do $1,900?"
   The question signals flexibility. The statement signals firmness.

3. Cites real data. "DAT shows $1.85/mile average on this lane."
   Brokers can argue with your opinion. They cannot argue with EIA.

4. Remembers every broker. Our AI knows Chris at Rakmark opens
   9% below where he settles and almost never actually walks away.

Happy to answer questions about the technical side.
